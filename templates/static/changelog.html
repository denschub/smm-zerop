2025-12-08

  • Due to a whole bunch of internal changes, your previously stored filters will no longer be restored. However, your picked filters are now part of the randomizer URL, which means you can just bookmark whatever set of filters you'd like to have applied. Or maybe you'll end up with multiple bookmarks with different filters, depending on your mood. We won't judge.

2024-10-12

  • You'll now be able to see each level's theme and its clear condition - if there is one.
